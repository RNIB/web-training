BURCHELL'S ZEBRA

Scientific name

Equus quagga burchellii

Appearance

White body with wide, dark stripes and a lot of variation in coat patterns between individuals. Males and females identical in appearance. Read more

Distribution

Spread throughout Southern Africa with the highest population in the Serengeti-Mara plains of Kenya and Tanzania.

Habitat

Prefers open grasslands, woodlands and scrub environments. Avoids dense forest, desert and wetland areas. Read more
